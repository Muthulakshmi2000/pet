about us

at whisker heaven, we believe every pet deserves a loving, forever home. our mission is to connect kind-hearted adopters with animals in need, offering a peaceful and compassionate space where second chances bloom.

founded by a team of animal lovers and rescue volunteers, whisker heaven was built on the belief that adoption should be simple, heartfelt, and welcoming. whether you're searching for a playful pup, a cuddly kitten, or a senior companion, we're here to help you find the perfect match.

with a soft touch and a big heart, we provide detailed pet profiles transparent adoption processes, and ongoing support - because finding a pet is just the beginning of a beautiful bond.

we're on a mission to support and empower people to responsibly rehome their pets

who we are, and why we created whisker heaven

whisker heaven has been set up by a group of animal lovers who are committed to ending pet homelessness and irresponsible rehoming practices. we're part of a registered chennai charity so you can rest assured that we always priorities pet welfare over profit.

3.7 million
pets from chennai homes have been given up in the past years according to the PFMA(pet food manufacturers association).

Lifestyle changes
both in living and work arrangements, plus financial obstacles are cited as the most common reasons for giving up a pet. whatever your reasons, we are here for you.

whisker heaven is here to help
by creating a pet adoption community where people who want to adopt safely connect with people who need to rehome a dog, cat or rabbit.

straightforward pet rehoming and adoption

whisker heaven is a new service that combines great technology with real people supporting you behind the scenes. our service connects potential adopters with people who need to rehome their pets. this makes it easier for good people to adopt the right pet whilst maximising the chance of pets Finding their forever home.

our charity offers a non-judgmental service to rehomers and gives them full, yet guided, control of the process.

we're also helping to reduce the number of pets going into shelters. this frees up space and resources for the pets awho have been abandoned, need immediate help or special care.
